What is PortalVR?

PortalVR is a mini YSWS where you create your own virtual or augmented reality experience. From magical portals to space worlds, you can build anything using Unity, WebXR, or Phone AR apps.

Build something awesome in ~10–15 hours, and we’ll ship you a real VR headset* to try it out!

(*Basic headset, not Meta Quest)

🌟 Inspirational Projects

🧠 VR Meditation Space

A peaceful, guided meditation room built in WebXR with calming soundscapes.

🚀 Space Explorer AR

Scan a table and launch planets into orbit using mobile AR and image tracking.

🏛️ VR History Tour

Walk through ancient Greece in VR, complete with 3D voice narration and hotspots.

🎁 What You Get

Complete your project and we’ll ship you a VR headset (up to $50) via Amazon.

It won’t be a Meta Quest, but you can test your creation in real VR! 🥽

❓ FAQ

How much time do I need to spend?

Around 10–15 hours is great! You can work solo or with a team.

Can I use my phone for AR?

Yes! You can use tools like 8thWall, Adobe Aero, or Unity AR Foundation to build phone-based AR.

Do I get to keep the headset?

Yes, it’s yours to keep and use for future projects!

Will it support SteamVR?

No, this is a basic mobile VR headset (no PC or 6DOF support).
